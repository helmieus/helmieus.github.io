Hello guys,

Today, I decided to take a break from the terminal and write a little about my exchange experience. Honestly, taking this opportunity is the best decision I've ever made during my degree. Those 4 months, absolutely the best. And yes, the program is during my final semester before I going for internship. So a bit chaotic laa cause I had my FYP going on. Just imagine how we try to have fun but everyone talking about FYP at the same time. Tapi apa-apapun, living abroad is like living in a dream. 

Since during my school time, I always said to mom, *Mak, kamek mok belajar kat France kelak.* And my mom just smile. As time goes by, my SPM is not that nice. No fail but cukup-cukup makan. And after my SPM, I took matriculation program for 2 years (PDT). During the second year, there's a scholarship offer from KPM to further their study abroad for 3 years if I'm not mistaken. And then after coming back to Malaysia, they need to serve as matriculation lecturer. But I don't want to be teacher so I didn't apply for the scholarship. Pup pap pup pap, laa masuk UMS🥲.

But that's okay, let me share my bittersweet during the program.

### First day in Indonesia

![flight](/assets/img/posts/2026-01-26-mobility/flight.jpg)

My parent company me to go to Semarang. Before We going to Semarang, we stay 1 night in Jakarta and take a train to Semarang on the next day. So we flew from Kuching to KL, then Jakarta. We arrived in Jakarta around 2200 WIB, so don't have any chance to explore Jakarta. On the next morning, we just had strawled around the hotel only then straight away to the train station. 
With train, the journey is 5 hours long and since that was my first train ride so I'm excited.
